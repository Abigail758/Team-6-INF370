About Us

GabriQ Group Pty Ltd is a construction company that renders professional services in Construction and General building, at competitive prices hence promoting long-term relationships with potential clients. The company is based in the Gauteng area and does offer their services to different parts of the South Africa. GabriQ Group is a 100% Black Owned Company, with a rating of BBBEE Level 1.
